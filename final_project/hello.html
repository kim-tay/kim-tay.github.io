Design is my jam. I love creating spaces- both virtual and in real life- that elicit feelings of warmth, familiarity, and connection.

As a photographer I have had opportunities to capture those feelings through the lens. On one such occasion my path lead me to Uganda where I connected with a Ugandan doctor who shares my passion for empowering people. Together we formed The Grow Hope Foundation (thegrowhopefoundation.org) which does just that in impoverished communities there. On the home front I have spent the past year transitioning from one life to another and am finding my feet as I enter the world of generating an income. I have recently completed a course on coding websites and plan to start my own business creating virtual spaces for clients who are looking for beautiful, clean, easy-to-navigate sites to engage their own clientele.

I am the mama of four incredible boys ranging from 11-24 and when I’m not adventuring through life with them can be found in Vancouver BC with the love of my life, often hiking, biking or road tripping. I am passionate about the outdoors, especially the beach and would spend my days there if given the opportunity. I am an avid wanderer and have traveled extensively with my camera capturing day-to-day life of different cultures.

I love laughter, empowering others to be their best selves, and am so grateful for my village because without them I wouldn’t be where I am today. And wine. I do love wine.
